Laguna Hills

Laguna Hills is a city in Orange County, California, that was incorporated in 1991. The city derives its name from the rolling hills and proximity to Laguna Canyon and Laguna Beach. However, the history of Laguna Hills goes back much further than its incorporation.

Laguna Niguel

Laguna Niguel is a city in Orange County, California, that has a rich and diverse history. The name of the city comes from the Spanish words for "lagoon" and "Niguili", which was the name of a Native American village that once existed near Aliso Creek.
