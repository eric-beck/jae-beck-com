Laguna Hills, California

Laguna Hills is a city in Orange County, California, that was incorporated in 1991. The city derives its name from the rolling hills and proximity to Laguna Canyon and Laguna Beach. However, the history of Laguna Hills goes back much further than its incorporation.

History

The area that is now Laguna Hills was originally inhabited by the Acjachemen Native Americans, who called it Niguili. They lived in villages along the creeks and springs, hunting, fishing, and gathering plants for food and medicine. The Acjachemen were one of the first peoples to encounter the Spanish explorers and missionaries who arrived in California in the 18th century.

The Spanish established Mission San Juan Capistrano in 1776 and began to convert and enslave the Acjachemen. The mission lands included the area of Laguna Hills, which was part of a large ranch called Rancho Niguel. The ranch was granted to Juan Avila in 1842, after Mexico gained independence from Spain. Avila was a prominent Californio who owned several other ranches in Southern California.

Avila's Rancho Niguel was one of the largest and most prosperous ranches in Orange County, covering over 13,000 acres of land. He raised cattle, horses and sheep, and grew wheat, barley, and grapes. He also built a large adobe house on a hill overlooking the ranch, which still stands today as a historical landmark.

Avila's family-owned Rancho Niguel until 1865, when they sold it to Lewis Moulton and Jean Pierre Daguerre, two American businessmen who expanded the ranching operations. They also leased some of their land to farmers who grew crops such as oranges, lemons, and walnuts. Moulton and Daguerre's partnership lasted until 1895, when Moulton bought out Daguerre's share and became the sole owner of Rancho Niguel.

Moulton continued to run Rancho Niguel until his death in 1938. His daughter, Louise Moulton Hanson, inherited the ranch and managed it with her husband Lewis Hanson. They faced many challenges during their tenure, such as droughts, floods, fires and economic depressions. They also witnessed the rapid urbanization of Orange County, as more people moved to the area after World War II.

In 1959, Louise Hanson sold part of Rancho Niguel to Cabot, Cabot and Forbes, a real estate development company that planned to build a master-planned community called Laguna Niguel. The community was designed to include residential neighborhoods, commercial centers, parks and schools. It also incorporated some of the historical features of Rancho Niguel, such as the adobe house and some of the original roads and trails.

Laguna Niguel became one of the first planned communities in California, and attracted many residents who were looking for a suburban lifestyle with scenic views and amenities. However, some residents were dissatisfied with the governance of Laguna Niguel by the county and wanted more local control over their affairs. They formed a movement to incorporate Laguna Niguel as a city.

The incorporation movement faced opposition from some neighboring communities, such as Laguna Hills and Aliso Viejo, which feared losing tax revenue and services from Laguna Niguel. They also wanted to incorporate themselves as separate cities. After several years of negotiations and lawsuits, a compromise was reached that allowed Laguna Niguel to incorporate in 1989, while Laguna Hills and Aliso Viejo would follow suit in 1991.

Laguna Hills became the 30th city in Orange County on December 20th, 1991. The city boundaries included parts of Rancho Niguel that were not annexed by Laguna Niguel, as well as parts of other neighboring communities such as Mission Viejo and Lake Forest. The city adopted its name from Laguna Canyon Road (now Alicia Parkway), which runs through its center.

Since its incorporation, Laguna Hills has developed into a thriving city with a population of over 30,000 people (as of 2010). The city offers a variety of services and facilities for its residents and visitors, such as parks, trails, libraries, museums, and shopping centers. The city also hosts several events throughout the year, such as the annual Memorial Day Half Marathon and the Fourth of July Celebration.

Laguna Hills is a city that celebrates its rich history and heritage, while also embracing its modern and diverse culture. It is a city that strives to provide a high quality of life for its people, while also preserving its natural beauty and environment. It is a city that is proud of its past, present, and future.
